What is a Pingspoofer?.

Pingspoofing is used in online gaming where a user intentionally introduces artificial delay to their network interface, causing their in-game ping to appear higher than it actually is. The term "ping" refers to the time it takes for data packets to travel from the user's device to the game server and back again. By manipulating their ping, a Pingspoofer can create the illusion of teleporting or lagging on the screens of other players in the game. A network interface serves as the bridge between a computer or device and a network. It is the network adapter you use to connect to your router.
